Vincent and Meryln founded an online grocery delivery service in 2023. The service, which is still in its early stages, offers convenient and affordable delivery of fresh produce and other groceries to customers in Nairobi, Kenya. The service is easy to use. Customers simply create an account on the website or app, select the groceries they want, and choose a delivery time. The service's team of personal shoppers will then assemble and deliver the groceries to the customer's door. The service is committed to providing customers with the freshest possible produce. The team of personal shoppers hand-selects all of the produce from local farmers and markets. The service also offers a satisfaction guarantee, so customers can be sure that they will be happy with their purchase. The service is still in its early stages, but it has already gained a loyal following among customers in Nairobi. The service is convenient, affordable, and offers high-quality produce. It is a great option for people who are looking for a hassle-free way to get their groceries.
